Sound files for the Catch the Falling Objects game:

1. catch.mp3 - Sound played when catching an apple
2. miss.mp3 - Sound played when an apple falls past the basket
3. bad_catch.mp3 - Sound played when catching a rock
4. bomb.mp3 - Sound played when catching a bomb
5. game_over.mp3 - Sound played when the game ends
6. music1.mp3 - Background music option 1
7. music2.mp3 - Background music option 2
8. music3.mp3 - Background music option 3

Note: The game will generate simple sounds if these files are not present.
You can replace these with your own sound files as long as you keep the same filenames.
